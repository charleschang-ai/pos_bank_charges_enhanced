Pos Bank Charges

POS terminals charge customers a transaction fee when collecting payments.

This module automates that process inside Odoo’s Point of Sale, ensuring accurate fee calculation, transparent billing, and seamless integration with your POS



Bank fees can be configured according to a percentage or a fixed amount.

Automatically generate bank service fee products.

When a customer pays using electronic payment methods (e.g., credit card, mobile payment), the POS automatically adds a configurable surcharge line to the order.

Works natively with Odoo’s POS interface without disrupting the cashier workflow. The surcharge appears instantly on the order and receipt.
